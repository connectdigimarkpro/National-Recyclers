How it Works

Our Recycling Process

We ensure that recycling is simple, transparent, and profitable. Follow our four simple steps to convert scrap materials to cash.

1

Request a Quote

Submit our website quote form, utilize our value estimator widget, or call 0411 932 620 to inform us about the scrap metal or alloy items you have.

2

Material Assessment

Our expert Malaga team will assess the type, purity, and grade of your materials to provide the highest possible market valuation based on WA pricing boards.

3

Collection or Drop-off

Choose between dropping off at our convenient 25 Finance Place, Malaga WA 6090, Australia depot, or utilizing our heavy commercial vehicle pickups with site bin replacements.

4

Fast Payment

Upon final weight assessment on our calibrated industrial weighbridges, we execute fast and secure electronic payments directly to your account.

Our Story

About National Recyclers

Established to address the rising demand for premium commercial waste processing, National Recyclers has grown to become a benchmark scrap metal and specialty alloy recycler in Western Australia. Headquartered in Malaga, our state-of-the-art facility processes hundreds of tonnes of recyclable materials weekly.

We focus heavily on partnering with manufacturing facilities, demolition companies, mining sectors, and construction contractors. By deploying modern scrap sorting equipment and environmentally sound procedures, we ensure maximum resource recovery while minimizing carbon footprints.
